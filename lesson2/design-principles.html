Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Contrast is one of the most important elements of design, and helps direct the viewer's focus as they engage with your work. It's also an easy way to set certain elements apart from the rest of the design, and really helps tell the story that you want to tell.

Heirarchy

Unity

unity.com

Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance. Designers structure visual characteristics—e.g., menu icons—so users can understand information easily. By laying out elements logically and strategically, designers influence users’ perceptions and guide them to desired actions. A strong visual hierarchy leads users to a page/screen’s functionality and gives them the right visual cues.

Rule of Thirds

Byu-Idaho

byui.edu

Here we can see the rule of thirds, a technique for composing visual images that proposes that important compositional elements should be placed along two equally spaced horizontal lines and two equally spaced vertical lines.
