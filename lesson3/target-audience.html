Who is the target audience?

TWith roots that date back to 1935, the Carbon County Chamber is a non-profit association that strives to be the indispensable resource for Carbon County businesses. Our Chamber is a voluntary organization of business owners, business people and citizens who are investing their time and money in a true community development program. We work together with the cities of East Carbon, Helper, Price, Wellington, along with Carbon County and the Carbon County Economic Development Department to improve the economic, civic, industrial and retail fortitude of the region, community, and the greater Caron County area. Any business leader or citizen who is interested in helping to develop the community is eligible to be a member of the Carbon County Chamber. The direct benefit is a stronger local economy, more businesses and more jobs with higher salaries and better benefits. The work of the chamber is financed by the investments of its members. The Chamber is guided by a group of 13 Board of Directors who are business leaders in the area and represent a cross-section of our chamber membership. Active membership in your chamber carries with it a sense of accomplishment and pride from the knowledge that you are doing your share of an important job for the community. Every member has a voice in determining the policies and projects that the chamber undertakes. Chamber membership pays when our members become involved in helping us increase their exposure within the community, assist in building relationships with other members, providing referrals, and offering opportunities for our members to enhance their business.
